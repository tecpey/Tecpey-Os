## The Problem

Financial markets are accessible to more people than at any point in history. The barrier is not access — it is preparation. Most retail market participants lose money not because markets are rigged, but because they enter without the knowledge, discipline, or emotional preparation to navigate volatility.

The Persian-speaking market — Iran, Afghanistan, Tajikistan, diaspora communities — has 120+ million people with high demand for financial education and extremely limited access to high-quality, native-language, responsible financial education.

**What exists today:**
- YouTube channels incentivized by signal sales
- Telegram groups mixing education with speculation
- Translated content with no cultural adaptation
- No standardized, verifiable educational credentials

**What does not exist:**
- A structured, curriculum-based financial education platform in Persian
- A behavioral competence assessment framework in Persian
- A verifiable, employer-recognized credential for financial literacy
- A safe practice environment connected to structured learning

---

## Our Thesis

Education first. Security always. Responsible market access.

TecPey is not a crypto exchange with an education section. It is an educational operating system for financial competence — with an exchange as one component.

We measure success not by trading volume, but by:
- Graduation rates through our 7-term curriculum
- Measurable behavioral improvement over time
- Long-term student retention (12+ months)
- Employment outcomes in the financial industry

## Our Commitments

**To our students:**
- We will never promise profit. We will never sell signals. We will never give investment advice.
- Your behavioral data belongs to you. It helps us help you. We never sell it.
- You can always export your data. Platform lock-in is a failure, not a strategy.

**To the market:**
- We compete on educational quality, not on user captivity.
- We are a school that happens to have an exchange, not an exchange that happens to have a school.

**To our team:**
- We build infrastructure that lasts. No shortcuts. No technical debt that goes unpaid.
- Persian is our source language. English is our translation. We serve our community first.

## What We Will Never Do

1. **Never promise profit.** No guarantee of returns. No signal services. No implied correlation between education and profit.
2. **Never surveil.** Behavioral data is used to help the student, not to be sold or shared without consent.
3. **Never gamify addiction.** The Trading Arena is a simulator. The Exchange is a tool. Neither is designed to maximize addictive behavior.
4. **Never trap users.** Export is always available. Lock-in through quality, not through data hostage-taking.
5. **Never use dark patterns.** No hidden fees. No artificially difficult cancellation. No manufactured scarcity.

**Phase 14 Strategic Document**
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-06-27
**Status:** Implementation-Ready

## Overview

TecPey Academy's reward system is designed around one principle: **reward what matters educationally, not what generates engagement metrics.**

Rewards must be earned by genuine learning and behavioral improvement. They must never be earned by:
- Spending time on the platform without learning
- Gaming the system through rapid quiz retakes
- Purchasing advantages
- Being lucky in the simulator

The reward system serves three purposes:
1. Reinforce behaviors that produce real learning
2. Recognize outstanding achievement
3. Build a culture of excellence within the TecPey Academy community

## Part 1 — XP (Experience Points / امتیاز تجربی)

### 1.1 XP Philosophy

XP is earned for completing educationally meaningful actions. XP cannot be purchased. XP cannot be earned by repeating actions beyond their learning value.

### 1.2 XP Earning Table

| Action | XP Awarded | Cap / Notes |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Complete a lesson (first time) | 30 XP | No cap |
| Pass a knowledge check (≥80%) | 10 XP | Once per check |
| Pass a module quiz (≥75%) | 50 XP | Once per quiz |
| Pass with ≥90% on module quiz | 80 XP (bonus) | Replaces base, once per quiz |
| Pass term midterm | 150 XP | Once per midterm |
| Pass term final exam | 300 XP | Once per exam |
| Term certificate earned | 500 XP | Once per term |
| Complete a flashcard review session (≥5 cards) | 10 XP | Once per day |
| Daily streak maintained | 15 XP/day | Doubles at day 30 |
| Simulator trade completed with full journal | 25 XP | Max 3/day (quality cap) |
| Simulator weekly challenge completed | 100 XP | Once per week |
| AI Mentor session (≥5 exchanges) | 15 XP | Once per day |
| Scenario training completed | 50 XP | Once per scenario |
| Peer mentoring session completed | 40 XP | Verified, max 2/week |

### 1.3 XP Anti-Gaming Rules

- Quiz retakes within 24 hours: 50% XP on pass
- Lesson re-reads after completion: 5 XP (vs 30 XP first completion)
- Simulator trades without journal: 0 XP
- Knowledge checks answered in under 10 seconds: 0 XP (too fast to read)
- Rapid flashcard sweeps (under 3 seconds per card): 0 XP

### 1.4 XP Cannot Be:

- Purchased
- Transferred between students
- Retroactively adjusted (except for system error corrections)
- Earned for social sharing, referrals, or non-educational actions

## Part 5 — Scholarships

### 5.1 Scholarship Philosophy

TecPey Academy offers merit-based scholarships to exceptional students who demonstrate learning quality and discipline — not financial need (that is a separate program). Scholarships recognize academic excellence.

### 5.2 Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for an Academic Excellence Scholarship:
- Term 3 certificate or higher
- Academic Score ≥ 85%
- Trading DNA Score ≥ 70
- Learning Consistency Score ≥ 80%
- Minimum 45 days enrollment

### 5.3 Scholarship Types

**Term Completion Scholarship**
Awarded to the top 5 students completing each term in a calendar quarter.
Value: Access to Advanced Track of choice (normally reserved for Term 7 graduates)
Awarded: Quarterly per term

**Behavioral Excellence Scholarship**
Awarded to students who achieve Trading DNA ≥ 90 for 60 consecutive days.
Value: Access to Professional Track mentorship session
Awarded: Rolling — when threshold met

**Full Academy Scholarship**
Awarded to one student per year who demonstrates the highest combined Academic + Trading DNA score across all 7 terms.
Value: TecPey Certified Professional (TCP) fast-track access + mentor partnership opportunity
Awarded: Annual

### 5.4 Scholarship Administration

- All scholarship decisions reviewed by the Academy Director
- Students notified in-platform and by email
- Scholarship recipients cannot receive the same scholarship twice
- Scholarships are non-transferable

## Part 6 — Top Students

### 6.1 Monthly Academic Leaders

Every month, TecPey identifies and recognizes the top students across each term level. Recognition includes:
- Featured position on the Academy Leaderboard
- Hall of Excellence profile feature (opt-in only)
- Top Student badge for the month

**Selection criteria:**
- Highest combined (Academic Score × 0.5 + Trading DNA × 0.5) during the month
- Minimum activity threshold (must have been active ≥ 15 days that month)
- Must not have been flagged for academic integrity violations

### 6.2 Hall of Fame

The TecPey Academy Hall of Fame is a permanent record of:
- All TCP graduates
- Annual Scholarship recipients
- Students who achieve Trading DNA ≥ 95 for 90 days
- Monthly Top Students (rolling 12-month display)

The Hall of Fame is public by default but can be opted out of.

## Part 9 — Prop Qualification

### 9.1 What is Prop Qualification?

TecPey Academy, in partnership with proprietary trading opportunities, offers a Prop Qualification pathway for graduates who meet the highest behavioral and academic standards.

**This is not a guarantee of prop firm funding.** It is a referral pathway based on demonstrated discipline and academic quality. Actual qualification decisions rest with partner institutions.

### 9.2 Prop Qualification Requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| All 7 terms completed | ✓ |
| TCP (TecPey Certified Professional) | ✓ |
| Trading DNA Score | ≥ 80 sustained for 60 days |
| Simulator trades | ≥ 200 documented |
| Rule Compliance Rate | ≥ 85% |
| FOMO Resistance Score | ≥ 80 |
| Revenge Trading Resistance | ≥ 80 |
| Journal Quality | ≥ 75% |
| Academic Score | ≥ 80% |

### 9.3 Prop Qualification Process

1. Student applies through Academy dashboard
2. AI Mentor generates a comprehensive behavioral report
3. Academy Director reviews and approves/declines referral
4. If approved, student receives a Prop Qualification certificate and partner referral

### 9.4 Prop Qualification Certificate Contents

The Prop Qualification certificate includes:
- All 12 Trading DNA dimension scores
- Academic score
- Total simulator trade count
- Behavioral summary (AI-generated, human-reviewed)
- Unique verification ID

## Part 10 — Gift Rules

### 10.1 Eligible Gifts

TecPey may offer tangible gifts to Academy top performers during special events (Academy Anniversary, National celebrations, etc.).

Eligible gifts:
- TecPey branded merchandise (books, notebooks, hardware)
- Access to premium features (Advanced Tracks, extended simulator capabilities)
- Educational materials (books, course access)
- Token rewards (ONLY as announced promotional programs — not as academic rewards)

Ineligible gifts:
- Cash payments for quiz performance (conflicts with responsible trading education)
- Discounts on live trading that create pressure to trade
- Any gift that could be construed as financial advice or investment recommendation

### 10.2 Gift Distribution Rules

- Gifts are announced in advance with clear eligibility criteria
- Gifts are delivered within 30 days of announcement
- Gifts are not substitutable for cash
- Gift eligibility is not retroactive (new criteria cannot apply to past performance)
- Gift programs cannot be conditional on exchange activity

## Part 11 — Fairness

### 11.1 Fairness Principles

1. **Transparent criteria:** All reward criteria are documented and public before any reward period begins.
2. **Equal access:** No reward is accessible only through paid features. All rewards are earnable through the free Academy path.
3. **No favoritism:** Reward decisions are algorithmic (for scored rewards) or multi-person reviewed (for manual awards).
4. **No retroactive changes:** Criteria cannot change mid-period.
5. **No pay-to-win:** XP, badges, certificates, and awards cannot be purchased.

### 11.2 Appeals Process

Students may appeal a reward decision within 14 days of the decision. Appeals are reviewed by the Academy Director. Decisions are communicated within 5 business days.

## Part 12 — Fraud Prevention

### 12.1 Known Fraud Vectors

| Fraud Type | Detection Method |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Quiz sharing (sharing answers) | Answer pattern analysis + IP/device correlation |
| Account sharing | Session overlap detection, behavior inconsistency |
| Bot-driven activity | Click pattern analysis, captcha on key actions |
| Simulator manipulation | Trade pattern anomaly detection |
| Journal copy-paste | Similarity analysis across submissions |
| Multiple accounts | Email, device, and IP correlation |

### 12.2 Automated Flags

The system automatically flags:
- Identical journal entries across different students
- Quiz response times consistently below human-possible thresholds
- Simulator trades that exactly match another student's entries (to the minute)
- XP accumulation curves that exceed natural human learning velocity

### 12.3 Consequences

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| Quiz cheating confirmed | Exam invalidated, 30-day ban from assessments | Account suspension |
| Account sharing | Rewards forfeited for the period | Account suspension |
| Multiple accounts | Secondary accounts terminated | Primary review |
| Journal fraud | Journal scores reset | Behavioral coaching required |

## Part 13 — Manual Review

### 13.1 When Manual Review is Required

Manual review is triggered for:
- Any certificate issued in less than 80% of the expected study time for that term
- Any Top Student or Award nomination where the student has fraud flags
- Any Prop Qualification application
- Any scholarship application
- Academic integrity appeals

### 13.2 Review Process

1. Automated flag generated
2. Assigned to Academy team member within 2 business days
3. Reviewed against platform activity data and journal quality
4. Decision communicated to student within 5 business days
5. If certificate is withheld pending review, student is notified immediately

## Executive Identity of TecPey

TecPey is **not** a crypto exchange with an education section attached.

**TecPey is a Digital Financial Education & Trading Operating System.**

It exists to close the preparation gap for people entering financial markets. The platform combines structured education, behavioral intelligence, safe practice environments, reputation systems, and responsible market access into one coherent product.

**Core Brand Promise (Permanent):**
> "تک‌پی، نقطه امن ورود به بازار رمزارز"  
> "TecPey — Your Safe Entry Point to the Crypto Market"

**Tagline Philosophy:**
Education first. Security always. Responsible market access.

## Product Philosophy

TecPey operates on a clear, non-negotiable hierarchy:

1. **Education-first** — The Academy is the primary entry point and trust engine.
2. **Trust before volume** — User growth and behavioral improvement are prioritized over trading activity.
3. **User growth before trading addiction** — The platform must never optimize for addictive or reckless trading behavior.
4. **Behavioral improvement before transaction count** — Success is measured by measurable improvement in Trading DNA dimensions, graduation rates, and long-term user competence.
5. **Revenue follows trust** — Monetization is a consequence of genuine value delivered, not the driver of product decisions.

TecPey serves multiple audiences through one integrated system:
- Individual learners (free Academy as zero-price-barrier entry)
- Educational institutions and companies
- Prop trading firms (behavioral screening and training)
- Enterprise and white-label clients (future)
- Developers and partners (future platform layer)

## Detailed Trading Arena Blueprint

Trading Arena is a **strategic pillar**, not a demo feature.

**Core Rules (Permanent):**
- Every eligible user receives 100,000 USD virtual starting capital.
- This is **Arena capital** only — not real, not withdrawable. It exists for evaluation, learning, practice, ranking, and qualification.
- Each user has exactly **3 total opportunities**:
  - Initial account
  - Recharge 1
  - Recharge 2
- If all 3 opportunities are lost, the user may purchase a renewal/extension package.
- Access is available through a monthly paid entry/subscription model (in addition to the 3 free opportunities).
- The Arena supports future seasons, leagues, rankings, challenges, tournaments, daily missions, achievements, ELO-style rating, performance scores, discipline scores, drawdown control, risk control, prop-style qualification paths, and advanced progression systems.

**Strategic Connections:**
- Deep integration with Academy (learning feeds performance, performance informs learning).
- Full recording and analysis by Mentor AI (Arena trades + real Exchange trades).
- Feeds into user Profile, Reputation, Trading DNA, and future Prop/Funded Account qualification.
- Major business engine (subscriptions, renewals) and retention engine.

**Future Capabilities (Architectural Reservation):**
- Advanced order types and realistic market simulation.
- Scenario training and historical replay.
- Team/organization competitions.
- Prop firm qualification pathways.
- Integration with real exchange for graduated users (with strict controls).

Trading Arena must never be designed to maximize addictive behavior. Risk awareness and behavioral improvement are primary goals.

## Detailed Mentor AI Blueprint

Mentor AI is **not a chatbot**. It is the **Trading Intelligence Engine** of TecPey OS.

**Core Responsibilities (Permanent):**
- Record and analyze **all** user trades in the Trading Arena.
- Record and analyze **all** user trades in the real Exchange (when connected).
- Build long-term trading memory for every user.
- Analyze: entries, exits, risk/reward, position sizing, drawdown, overtrading, revenge trading, FOMO, discipline, emotional patterns, consistency, learning gaps, and improvement over time.
- Generate: personalized feedback, trading journals, weekly/monthly reports, learning paths, targeted exercises, warnings, and growth recommendations.
- Connect Academy learning behavior with Arena performance and Exchange behavior.
- Help users improve their competence and reduce harmful patterns — never push excessive trading.

**Principles:**
- Explainable, ethical, and risk-aware at all times.
- No financial advice or profit guarantees.
- User data is used to help the user, not to sell or exploit.
- Must integrate with Academy progress, Trading DNA, and reputation systems.

Mentor AI is a major retention and differentiation engine. It must evolve from single-agent to part of the broader AI Operating System while preserving its core educational mission.

## Academy → Arena → Exchange Journey

**Primary User Journey (Permanent Structure):**

1. **Visitor** → Discovers via SEO, GEO, word-of-mouth, or referral.
2. **Academy (Free Level 1 / Entry Education)** → Structured learning, quizzes, certificates.
3. **Exam & Scoring** → Mastery gating, performance measurement.
4. **Trading Arena** → Virtual capital, practice, evaluation, behavioral data collection.
5. **Mentor AI Evaluation** → Analysis of learning + Arena behavior.
6. **Reputation / Profile** → Trading DNA, scores, badges, history.
7. **Exchange** → Responsible real-market access (for qualified or interested users).
8. **Advanced Arena / Prop Qualification** → Higher-stakes simulation, prop-style pathways.
9. **Financial Ecosystem** → Future products and services.

Top Academy performers may receive scholarships, advanced access, funded-style opportunities, and initial trading support.

The journey must always present the Academy as a safe, parallel, and equally important path to the Exchange.

## ۱. مقدمه / Introduction

This document is the official revenue registry for TecPey. Every current and future revenue stream is catalogued here with purpose, target user, dependencies, priority, launch phase, risk, and estimated strategic value.

**Principle:** Revenue follows trust. Trust follows genuine educational value. No revenue stream ships that compromises educational integrity for monetization.

**اصل:** درآمد از اعتماد می‌آید. اعتماد از ارزش آموزشی واقعی. هیچ جریان درآمدی نباید یکپارچگی آموزشی را قربانی کند.

## 5. Strategic Principles

1. **Education first.** No revenue stream may compromise educational integrity. Free Academy is permanent.
2. **Trust before monetization.** New revenue streams are introduced only after trust is established.
3. **Value-based pricing.** Price reflects value delivered, not what the market will bear.
4. **Transparency.** All fees disclosed upfront. No hidden charges, no dark patterns.
5. **Revenue diversification.** No single revenue stream exceeds 50% of total revenue (target).
6. **Ethical data use.** User data is never the product. Anonymized, consented data products only.

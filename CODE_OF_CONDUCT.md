# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

TecPey is a professional platform serving the Persian-speaking crypto community. Everyone who contributes to or interacts with this project — employees, contractors, or community members — is expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and respect.

---

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Communicate professionally and respectfully
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the platform and its users
- Show empathy toward colleagues and users
- Keep discussions technical and productive
- Protect the privacy and security of user data at all times

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sharing confidential TecPey code, data, or business information externally
- Using the platform or codebase for unauthorized financial gain
- Deliberate introduction of security vulnerabilities
- Misleading users about financial risks or product capabilities

---

## Financial Content Standards

Given that TecPey operates in the crypto financial space, contributors must additionally:

- Never create content that implies guaranteed profit or investment returns
- Never write code that bypasses risk warnings for users
- Never build features that encourage impulsive or uneducated trading
- Always prioritize user safety over engagement metrics

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:

1. **Warning** — for minor or first-time violations
2. **Temporary suspension** — from contributions or access
3. **Permanent removal** — from the project and all associated systems

Reports are handled confidentially by TechnoPardakht leadership.

---

## Reporting

To report a violation, email: **info@tecpey.ir**

Include a description of the incident, relevant dates, and any supporting evidence. All reports are treated with confidentiality.
